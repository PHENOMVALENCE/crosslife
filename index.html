About

CrossLife Mission Network

CrossLife Mission Network (CMN) is a non-denominational and inter-denominational Christian ministry based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We exist as a community of Life, Love, Sonship, and Prayer, welcoming people from diverse backgrounds, ages, and walks of life.

Our Mandate

To manifest Sons of God who know who they are in Christ, and what Christ can do through them.

Our Vision

To be a global network of the manifested Sons of God walking in the Life of God, expressing the New Creation realities through experiential knowledge, and advancing the Eternal Ordinations of Christ across the nations with one message, one mandate, and one mission.

Our Mission

To reach the global network by revealing Christ as the Way, the Truth, and the Life; equipping Sons of God to serve effectively in the Kingdom of God through discipleship training, revelational teaching of the (Gospel (s) of the Cross, Glorification, Kingdom of God, and Immortality), spiritual relevancy formation, and life-filled koinonia—manifesting His Spirit, Love, Life, and Power in the Church and the marketplace.

Our Convictions

Statement of Faith | Our Convictions | Our Beliefs

CrossLife Mission Network (CMN) is founded on the following doctrinal convictions that uphold the Gospel of Grace, the mandate of Revival, and the influence of the Gospel in the Marketplace

The Scriptures

We believe that the Holy Bible is the inspired, authoritative, and infallible Word of God—profitable for doctrine, reproof, correction, and training in righteousness. It is the final authority for life, faith, practice, and the expression of Christ in every sphere, including the marketplace.

(2 Timothy 3:16–17; Hebrews 4:12; Psalm 119:105; Matthew 4:4)

The Triune God

We believe in one eternal God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—co-equal and co-existent, Creator of all things, and sovereign over the affairs of men. His redemptive purpose is revealed through His desire to restore humanity into His Life and glory.

(Deuteronomy 6:4; Matthew 28:19; Genesis 1:1; Ephesians 1:9–10)

The Lord Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, ascension, and eternal lordship. He is the only way to salvation, the expression of the Life of God, and the head of His Church.

(John 1:1–4, 14; Isaiah 7:14; Hebrews 4:15; 1 Corinthians 15:3–4; Acts 1:9; Colossians 1:18; John 14:6)

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the person, presence, and power of the Holy Spirit. He indwells, fills, teaches, convicts, empowers, and imparts spiritual gifts for ministry. He is the catalyst of Revival and the revealer of Christ in the believer.

(John 14:16–17, 26; Acts 1:8; Acts 2:1–4; 1 Corinthians 12:4–11; Galatians 5:22–23)

The Church

We believe that the Church is the Body of Christ—called to reveal His Life, extend His Kingdom, and disciple nations. The Church is commissioned to be a transformational voice in every sphere of society.

(Ephesians 1:22–23; Matthew 28:18–20; Acts 2:42–47; Matthew 5:13–16)

Salvation by Grace

We believe that salvation is a free gift of God, received by grace through faith in Christ alone. Through His finished work, believers are forgiven, justified, reconciled to God, and empowered to live in Gospel freedom.

(Ephesians 2:8–9; Romans 3:24; Romans 5:1; Colossians 1:13–14; John 19:30)

New Creation Realities

We believe that those who receive Christ are regenerated into the New Creation— born of God, indwelt by His Life, and called to manifest His nature through sonship, righteousness, holiness, and dominion.

(2 Corinthians 5:17; John 1:12–13; Romans 8:10–11; Romans 8:14–19; Ephesians 4:24)

Kingdom Influence

We believe that God’s Kingdom is present and advancing through His people. As Sons of God, believers are called to influence culture, transform systems, and establish righteousness in the earth.

(Matthew 6:10; Romans 8:19; Matthew 5:13–16; Daniel 7:27; Habakkuk 2:14)

Gospel in the Marketplace

We believe the Gospel is a mandate to disciple nations and influence systems. Believers carry the Life of Christ into business, leadership, culture, and societal transformation as salt and light.

(Genesis 1:26–28; Matthew 5:13–16; Colossians 3:23–24; Revelation 11:15)

Meet Our Senior Pastor

Pastor Lenhard Kyamba

Pastor Lenhard Kyamba (PLK) is a Minister of Life leading an army of Sons of God through CrossLife Mission Network, the School of Christ, and the Kingdom Business Forum—with a mandate to manifest Sons of God who know their identity in Christ. He is a practicing Advocate (Corporate, International Commerce & Maritime Law), holds a Master's in International Trade and Maritime Law and a BA in Theology and Leadership, and is author of Awakened Person, Manifesting Sons of God, Kingdom Profile, Man Awake, Woman Awake, among other Kingdom resources.
